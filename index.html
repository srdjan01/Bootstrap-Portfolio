About Me

My name is Srdjan Markovich. I was born in Bosnia and immigrated to the United States a little over fifteen years ago with my family. We came to this country due to civil unrest that was within my country and surrounding territories. In my short time here, I have had the opportunity to do and experience many things.

When I am not working as a System Engineer at Intel or studying full stack web development, I enjoy spending time with my friends and squeezing in as many activities as I can. Some of these activities are going to the gym, practicing yoga, biking local trails and entertaining my two young Siberian Huskies: Noni and Sia. I also enjoy traveling and visiting locations that have nice beaches that I can lay on without care for hours on end. My favorite beaches that I have visited since being in the United States have been on Maui, Hawaii and Cancun, Mexico.

My goal is to complete the full stack web development bootcamp and move within Intel to a position that I can use these skills. Intel is large and there is endless opportunity for me within the organization to use these skills and further develop my career. I am hopeful that I will be doing this for some time to come.
